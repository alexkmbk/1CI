This list is used to manage user settings you need to copy or clear.

The list consists of three tabs:

  • Appearance - settings controlling the appearance of application desktop, forms (application lists, etc), and command interface.
  • Report settings - user-defined report settings, grouped by report option name.
  • Other settings - user's personal settings, spreadsheet document printing settings, settings for quick access to additional reports and data processors, Favorites section settings, and other settings not included in other sections.

Managing settings

  • To enable the settings you need, select the corresponding check boxes. You can also:
    • Click to Mark all settings;
    • Click to Clear mark for all settings;
  • To copy or clear settings, click Select.

Viewing settings

  • To view the report settings or appearance settings, double-click them or use the All actions - Open command).
